New England Computational Biology Symposium
October 1–2, 2026 · Microsoft Research New England, Cambridge, MA

About

A two-day, in-person symposium bringing the New England computational biology community together — keynotes, selected talks, lightning talks, open-problem sessions, and posters. Our focus is local networking, trainee visibility, and seeding new collaborations at the frontier of computation and the life sciences.

🧬
Generative AI · Virtual Cells · Protein Design
Foundation models for biology, virtual cell simulation, and computational protein design.
🔬
Spatial & Single-Cell Omics
Methods, models, and applications at the single-cell and spatial transcriptomics frontier.
🤖
Agentic AI for Biology
Autonomous agents, reasoning systems, and agent–scientist collaboration for discovery.
