About the symposium

NECB 2026 brings together the New England computational biology community for two days of keynotes, contributed talks, open-problem sessions, tutorials, and posters at Microsoft Research New England.

We are an in-person, locally rooted symposium with a few simple goals: make it easy for researchers across New England universities, hospitals, and institutes to meet each other; create visibility for junior researchers and trainees; and seed new collaborations at the frontier of computation and the life sciences.

Registration is kept intentionally affordable at $100 per attendee. We plan to recognize outstanding poster presentations.
